Bulletins

This week was a wonderful and a fruitful week

Good day parents, This week, our students had so much fun in class. We learned many new facts about different animals and expanded our vocabulary. Our student shared stories about what they did on the weekend with their parents. We made lots of crafts, learned about spring and the animals that appear during the season and learned many new songs related to spring. Please visit the following links and have your kids sing with you.

  • Springtime Sunshine
  • Spring Song for Children - "Springtime"
  • Spring is Here with Lyrics - Kids Songs by The Learning Station
  • April | Calendar Song for Kids | Month of the Year Song | Jack Hartmann
  • 5 Spring Flowers | Treeschoolers | Two Little Hands TV
  • Hungry Caterpillar with Lyrics
We read many books on friendship, kindness and appropriate classroom behavior. These include: “How to be a friend” by Laurie Krasny Brown and Mark Brown, “I Just Want To Do It My Way!” by Julia Cook, “But It’s Not My Fault” by Julia Cook, “I Can’t Believe You Said That!” by Julia Cook, “ Mitzi’s Mitzvah” by Gloria Kosher. We learned the letter P and the letter B. We sang songs about each letter and learned about pigs and bees. We were lucky to go outside for recess this week. The kids had so much fun playing outside in the sunshine. We made bubbles, played with chalk, and climbed on the play structure. Have a wonderful weekend.
